Pizza Dough

Description

Make perfect pizza at home with this classic homemade pizza recipe, including a pizza dough recipe, topping suggestions, and step-by-step instructions with photos.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups (355ml) warm water
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 (490g) "00" flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons see salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. Proof the yeast
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough
  3. Let the dough rise
  4. Preheat the oven and make portion the dough in pizza-balls
  5. Flatten the dough ball, stretch out into a round, and add favourite toppings
  6. Bake it in a pre-heated oven for about 10 minutes
